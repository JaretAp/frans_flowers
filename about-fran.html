Hi, I’m Fran. I grew up learning flowers in my grandmother’s city garden and at a neighborhood farmers’ market. Those early mornings taught me seasonality, color, and how flowers can say what words can’t. After years of arranging for friends and community events, I opened Fran’s Flowers to bring that everyday magic to our town.

At Fran’s Flowers, we believe great arrangements start with what’s in season—fresh stems that look better, last longer, and reflect the moment. Every order begins with a conversation about who it’s for and how it should feel; design isn’t a template, it’s a translation of sentiment into color, texture, and shape. We care about our footprint, favor local growers when possible, and keep pricing straightforward.
